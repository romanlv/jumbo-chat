# Jumbo88 Troubleshooting Guide

## Location and Access Issues

Jumbo88 uses your device's location to verify you are in an eligible state. If you see a message saying Jumbo88 is not available in your area, try the following steps:

1. Make sure your VPN is turned off. VPNs can make it look like you are in a different location, which may block access even if you are in an eligible state.
2. Enable location services in your browser or device settings and allow Jumbo88 to access your location.
3. Try a different browser. Some browsers block location access by default.
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page.
5. If you are on a mobile device, make sure GPS is enabled and try again.

If you have tried all of these steps and are still seeing the error, you may be in a restricted state. Jumbo88 is not available in the following states: California, Connecticut, Delaware, Idaho, Illinois, Louisiana, Maryland, Michigan, Montana, Nevada, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Tennessee, Washington, and West Virginia.

## Login Issues

If you cannot log in or the login page keeps spinning:

1. Refresh the page and try again.
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
3. Try a different browser or use an incognito/private window.
4. If you forgot your password, click "Login" and then "Forgot Password?" to receive a reset link by email.
5. Make sure you are using the correct email address associated with your account.

If none of these steps work, your account may need attention from a human agent.

## Game Freezing or Not Loading

If a game freezes or the page does not load:

1. Refresh the page.
2. Clear your browser cache.
3. Try a different browser.
4. Check your internet connection.
5. If you are on mobile, try closing and reopening the browser app.

If the issue continues after trying these steps, contact support with a screenshot or description of what happened.

## Payment and Redemption Issues

If you are having trouble purchasing Gold Coins or redeeming Sweepstakes Coins:

1. Make sure you have completed identity verification. First-time redemptions require ID verification before processing.
2. Check that you meet the minimum redemption threshold of 100 Sweepstakes Coins.
3. Verify your payment method is valid and up to date.
4. Redemptions are typically processed within 24 hours. If it has been longer, contact support.

For specific issues with a pending transaction or missing coins, contact a human support agent who can look into your account.
